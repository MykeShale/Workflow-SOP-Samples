# SkyLution Client Handover Guide: TawiLend Platform

**Welcome to your new Loan Management System!** 
This guide provides a quick overview to get your team up and running on the TawiLend platform.

## What Has Changed?
We have successfully transitioned your operations from manual spreadsheets and paper forms to a fully integrated, cloud-based platform. 
* **Borrowers** now apply online via the Member Portal.
* **Staff** manage everything from a centralized Admin Dashboard.
* **Approvals & Disbursements** are now guided by automated rules and API integrations.

## Quick Start URLs
* **Member Portal (For your clients)**: `https://portal.yourdomain.com`
* **Admin Dashboard (For your staff)**: `https://admin.yourdomain.com`

## Key Administrator Actions

### Adding New Staff Members
1. Log into the Admin Dashboard.
2. Go to **Settings** > **User Management**.
3. Click **Add User**, enter their email, and assign a Role (Loan Officer, Manager, Admin). They will receive a setup email automatically.

### Adjusting Loan Products & Interest Rates
1. Go to **Configuration** > **Loan Products**.
2. Select the product you wish to modify.
3. Update the interest rate, term limits, or fee structures. *Note: Changes only apply to new applications, not existing active loans.*

## Getting Support
Your success is our priority. If you encounter any technical issues or have questions about the system:
* **Knowledge Base**: Click the "Help" icon in the bottom left of your Admin Dashboard for video tutorials and FAQs.
* **Technical Support**: Email `support@skylution.com` or call your dedicated account manager at `1-800-SKY-LUTN`.
* **Urgent Outages**: Please use the emergency pager number provided in your SLA agreement.
