# SkyLution Client Handover Guide: BomaSuite

**Welcome to your automated property management platform!**
This guide is designed to help your team transition smoothly into using BomaSuite for daily operations.

## What Has Changed?
We've replaced manual tracking and paper processes with a unified digital platform.
* **Tenants** have a self-service portal for paying rent via M-Pesa, submitting maintenance requests, and accessing their lease.
* **Your Staff** (Managers, Caretakers, Storekeepers, Technical) have role-based access to specific properties and dashboards.
* **Accounting** is now automated, with M-Pesa C2B integration routing payments directly and auto-updating the ledger in real-time.

## Quick Start URLs
* **Tenant Portal**: `https://portal.bomasuite.com`
* **Staff Dashboard**: `https://manage.bomasuite.com`

## Key Administrator Actions

### Onboarding Staff
1. Log into the Staff Dashboard as an Admin.
2. Go to **Settings** > **Staff**.
3. Click **Add Staff**, enter their email, assign a role (e.g., `caretaker`), and link them to specific properties.
4. They will receive an email invite to set their password (optionally enabling SMS OTP / 2FA).

### Onboarding a New Tenant
1. Go to **Properties** > Select the specific Unit.
2. Click **Add Lease**, enter the tenant's email and lease terms.
3. Click **Send Invitation**. The system will email them a link to set up their portal user account and digitally sign the lease.

### Managing Monthly Billing
* **Water Invoices**: Caretakers enter water meter readings at the end of the month. The system calculates consumption and adds it to the monthly invoice automatically.
* **Rent Generation**: The system auto-generates rent invoices on the 1st of every month.

## System Maintenance & Support
* **Help Center**: Access step-by-step guides and video tutorials via the Help menu in your dashboard.
* **Technical Support**: For system errors or bugs, email `support@skylution.com`.
* **Security Check**: Your dashboard tracks recent login devices and triggers email alerts on new unrecognized logins for security.
